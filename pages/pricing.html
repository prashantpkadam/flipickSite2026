Pricing FAQ

The questions buyers ask first.

What is a credit worth?

One credit equals ₹1 under the current plan. Pricing throughout the platform is shown in credits so creation and distribution share one balance; the calculator converts any campaign to both credits and rupees.

Can I buy creation without distribution — or the other way around?

Yes. The two products draw from the same credit balance but are independent. Create video and export it, distribute a film you already have, or use both together as one workflow.

How does the per-recipient rate work?

Distribution is priced per recipient, and the rate is set by the band your campaign's total recipient count falls into — from 100 credits each under 100 recipients down to 40 credits each above 10,000. Larger sends are cheaper per person.

Why do edits change the creation cost?

Base production is 3,300 credits per finished minute. More editing and regeneration consumes more credits, which is why approving the screenplay and storyboard first keeps most projects in the lower tiers — revisions land before rendering, not after.

Do credits expire, and is there a free trial?

Credit terms and trial availability depend on your plan — the quickest way to confirm what applies to you is to book a demo or talk to sales, and we'll set you up with a balance to test a first project.
