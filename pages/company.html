Our story

Built on 15 years in learning technology.

Flipick didn't start with AI video — it started in learning technology. Since 2011, Flipick LMS has helped enterprises deliver, manage and track learning at scale.

Working inside those programmes, we kept hearing the same need: training and communication had to become video — studio-grade, personalized and measurable — without the cost and timelines of a production house. So we built the Flipick AI video platform on the foundation of 15 years of learning-technology experience.

Today, the same company that runs your courses can create the video inside them, deliver it personally, and prove it landed.

Founded by Ravi Dugal  ·  Built and run from Pune, India  ·  Trusted by Pearson, IIBF, IICA and more
