FAQ

Common questions about Flipick's AI Video Platform.

What is the Flipick AI Video Platform?

Flipick is a studio-grade AI video creation platform that lets L&D, marketing and customer teams create, personalise and prove video end to end — from a single pipeline that covers every content type and industry.

Which teams use Flipick?

L&D and training teams ship multilingual, assessable courses; marketing teams produce on-brand ads and promos at calendar speed; and customer education teams send personalised walkthroughs and onboarding videos — all from the same platform.

What types of video can I create?

Training videos (structured, multilingual, assessable), ads and promos (campaign-ready, re-skinnable), narrative films (consistent cast across scenes), and explainer videos (clear how-tos with tracked next steps).

Which industries does Flipick serve?

Manufacturing (safety and SOP training), retail (frontline onboarding and promos), BFSI (compliance with audit-ready tracking), food and beverage (franchise training), software (product launches and explainers), and healthcare (compliance and patient education).

How does Flipick personalise videos at scale?

Flipick delivers a unique version of each video per recipient using variable data fields — names, products, prices and offers swap automatically without re-rendering. Every view is authenticated, so you get verified completion data, not just view counts.
