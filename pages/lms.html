What G2 does

Eight ways AI runs across the learning lifecycle.

1

AI-powered assistance

A knowledge-grounded assistant, grounded only in your content.

  • RAG assistant answers only from verified course materials, transcripts and resources
  • Every answer carries citations — no hallucination
  • Dynamic indexing of HTML content and auto-transcribed video makes everything searchable
2

Advanced analytics

Dashboards for learners, managers and administrators.

  • Multi-layered progress — own journey, department trends, org-wide insight
  • Talent-gap analysis against required competencies, at scale
  • Surfaces “70% of junior developers lack advanced cloud skills” automatically
3

Intelligent question banks

Generate assessments straight from your content.

  • Instantly create large question pools from uploaded material
  • MCQ, case-based and scenario formats
  • Tagged by difficulty and category for adaptive delivery
4

Smarter course creation

Create faster, on-brand and on-framework.

  • Auto-generated course thumbnails aligned to brand aesthetics
  • Build content from AI prompts tied to SSF role definitions
  • Courses aligned to industry standards and skill taxonomies
5

Skills-framework integration

Map every learner to a real role.

  • Integrates SSF sectors, occupations and roles into structured pathways
  • Maps each learner’s profile to relevant roles and auto-finds gaps
  • Role-migration planning for career progression
6

Next-gen assessments

Adaptive, exam-grade evaluation.

  • Difficulty adjusts in real time to response accuracy
  • GMAT-style: fair but challenging
  • Instant diagnostics of strengths and weaknesses
7

Adaptive learning

A unique path for every learner.

  • An assessment generates a customised pathway
  • Recommends relevant existing modules
  • Dynamically generates new micro-content where gaps remain
8

Learning goals

Career-aligned upskilling journeys.

  • Learners set a target role and timeframe
  • SSF-powered role and competency mapping
  • Uses existing content; generates new modules for gaps
