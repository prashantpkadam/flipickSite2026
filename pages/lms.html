FAQ

Common questions about Flipick LMS.

What is Flipick LMS G2?

Flipick LMS G2 is an AI skills-development platform that puts AI across the whole learning lifecycle — content creation, learner engagement, adaptive assessment and analytics — and aligns every learner to real industry roles through frameworks like the Singapore Skills Framework.

How does the zero-hallucination learning assistant work?

The RAG-based assistant answers only from your verified course materials, transcripts and resources. Every response carries citations back to the source document, so there is no risk of invented or off-policy information reaching learners.

What is adaptive assessment in Flipick LMS?

Flipick LMS uses GMAT-style adaptive testing where question difficulty adjusts in real time based on each learner’s responses. The result is a precise competency score — not just pass or fail — with instant diagnostics of strengths and weaknesses.

How does skills-framework integration work?

The platform integrates the Singapore Skills Framework’s sectors, occupations and roles into structured learning pathways. Each learner’s profile is mapped to relevant roles, gaps are identified automatically, and role-migration planning supports career progression.

Can Flipick LMS generate courses and assessments automatically?

Yes. You can build courses from AI prompts tied to role definitions, auto-generate large question pools from uploaded material in MCQ, case-based and scenario formats, and create personalised learning paths that update dynamically as the learner progresses.
