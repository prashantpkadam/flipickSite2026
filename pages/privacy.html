Section 01

Our roles

For personal data of our own users (account holders and website visitors), Flipick is the controller / Data Fiduciary. For Recipient Data that Customers upload to personalize and distribute video, the Customer is the controller / Data Fiduciary and Flipick acts as a processor / Data Processor on the Customer's instructions.

Flipick users

Flipick is the controller

We determine how account and usage data is processed for account holders and website visitors.

Recipient Data

Customer is the controller

You control Recipient Data. Flipick processes it only on your documented instructions to provide the Service.

Section 02

Personal data we collect

A
Account & business data
Name, work email, company, role and billing details.
C
Customer Content
Briefs, scripts, brand assets, images, audio and Asset Library Elements, which may include images or voices of individuals you have rights to use.
R
Recipient Data
Names and personalization fields, and contact identifiers such as email addresses and phone/WhatsApp numbers, plus delivery and viewing status.
V
Viewer & authentication data
Information used to verify a viewer's identity before playback, and engagement analytics (views, completion, clicks).
U
Usage, device & log data
IP address, browser and device information, actions taken, and cookies or similar technologies.
P
Payment data
Processed by our payment provider; we do not store full card numbers.
Section 03

How we use personal data

  • Provide, operate and secure the Service;
  • Generate, personalize and deliver video, and produce proof analytics;
  • Verify viewers where authentication is enabled;
  • Manage billing, accounts and support;
  • Prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal obligations; and
  • Troubleshoot and improve the Service.
AI training: We do not use Customer Content or Recipient Data to train generative AI models except within your own tenant.
Section 04

Legal bases

We process personal data on one or more lawful bases: your consent; performance of a contract; compliance with legal obligations; and our legitimate interests (such as securing and improving the Service). Under the DPDP Act, we process personal data for lawful purposes with your consent or other permitted legitimate uses.

Section 05

How AI processing works

Generated Output is produced by AI models from your inputs. Inputs are processed to create your output. Generated Output may not be unique and should be reviewed before use.

Section 06

Sharing and sub-processors

We share personal data with:

  • Service providers acting on our behalf (cloud hosting, AI model providers, messaging/email/WhatsApp delivery, analytics, and payment);
  • Customers (for Recipient Data they control);
  • Authorities where required by law; and
  • Parties to a corporate transaction.

We require processors to protect personal data. A current list of sub-processors is available on request.

Section 07

International transfers

We may process data outside your country. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards for such transfers in accordance with applicable data-protection law.

Section 08

Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate or legal purposes, after which we delete or anonymise it. Recipient Data is retained per the Customer's instructions.

Section 09

Security

We use technical and organisational measures such as encryption in transit and at rest, access controls and monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Section 10

Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction, completion, updating or erasure of your personal data, withdraw consent, and raise a grievance. The DPDP Act also provides a right to nominate; the GDPR additionally provides rights to portability and to object.

To exercise your rights, contact privacy@flipick.com.

If you are a Recipient, please contact the Customer who sent you the video (the controller); we will assist them in responding to your request.
