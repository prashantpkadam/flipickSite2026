Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Flipick.

What is Flipick?

Flipick is an AI video platform that creates studio-grade video, distributes a personalised version to every viewer via Variable Video Production (VVP), and proves who watched with authenticated viewer tracking. One platform covers the full create → distribute → prove pipeline.

What is Variable Video Production (VVP)?

Variable Video Production is Flipick’s method for generating personalised videos at scale. You create one master film with variable data fields, then the platform swaps names, products, prices and other details per recipient — producing a unique version for every viewer without re-rendering from scratch.

How much does Flipick cost?

Everything is priced in credits — 1 credit = ₹1. AI Video Creation starts at 3,300 credits per finished minute (typical projects run 5,000–5,500 with moderate edits). VVP Distribution ranges from 50 credits per recipient (under 100) down to 20 per recipient (above 10,000). No seats, no subscriptions.

Which teams use Flipick?

L&D and training teams ship multilingual, assessable courses; marketing teams produce on-brand ads and promos at calendar speed; and customer education teams send personalised walkthroughs and onboarding videos — all from the same platform.

How is Flipick different from other AI video tools?

Most AI video tools generate a clip from a prompt. Flipick runs a full studio pipeline — screenplay, storyboard approval, consistent characters and products across scenes, brand overlays, personalised distribution, and authenticated viewer analytics. It’s built for teams that need on-brand, measurable video at scale, not one-off clips.
